WELCOME TO C. K. PATEL TECHNICAL INSTITUTE.

Established in 1983 in Nar Town the industrial training centre was started from the donation made by elite villegers to build a strong and skilled community. The objective of the centre is to train young boys and girls to acquire vocational skills and thus be self-employed in the following sixteen trades. Duration of the training ranges from one year to two years and eligibility for admission is 10th class pass (High School), in most cases.
